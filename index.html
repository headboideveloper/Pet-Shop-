WHY DOGS?

Dogs are the most amazing pets to keep around. They are one of the few animals that understand trust, friendship and family above all, and this helps them build a genuine relationship with the households they find themselves and even amongst themselves.

Security

All dog breeds offer protection to the household they find themselves and aid relief of theft and loss to their owners

Friendship

Dogs are best human friends. They seek to understand and ease the burden of their housemates all the time.

Loyalty

A vaste majority of dog breeds show affection and care to their owners and are ready to put their lives just to save one from harm.

BREEDS WE OFFER
